# nmap

Purpose:
*Used to scan hosts and discover open ports.*

Examples:

`nmap localhost`
Scan my local machine.

`nmap 192.168.1.1`
Scan another device.

`nmap -sV localhost`
Show service versions.

My Notes:
*One of the coolest networking tools I have used so far. I used it to check my own router and my own devices and its really cool*
